About Indurate

Indurate is a free fitness and social media app. It serves as a multifaceted platform for
socializing, tracking workouts, scheduling fitness routines, and much more. 

Our
constantly advancing features give you the tools to express yourself in
innovative ways and share your methods. 

We are dedicated to a secure and encouraging community that welcomes everyone.
